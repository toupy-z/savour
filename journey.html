This is our journey!

Savour was created

November 2024

Everyone was given a role and had to complete weekly tasks to forward the company. This included designing the logo, slogan, and the product.

Bake sale

December 2024

In order to raise money, we held a bake sale at our school. We sold cupcakes, muffins and cookies to the younger years. We raised over £120!

Year 7 Competition

January 2025

Year 7 students can recycle used crisp packets (or foil-lined packets) in their form's collection box. After informing their teacher, they earn a merit point per packet. The top three contributors in each form can win badges, stickers, or merchandise, while the top form wins chocolates, biscuits, or halal sweets.

Winter Fair

23rd January 2025

We did another fundraiser at our school's winter fair. For a small cost of 50p, students could pick any raffle ticket from a tombola, with a chance to win prizes ranging from card games to a churro maker. We gained about £70!

Valentines Day

14th February 2025

We hosted a Valentine's Day event featuring a bake sale and a rose delivery service. Students could purchase delicious treats and send a rose to friends, crushes, or loved ones, along with a personalized note or song dedication. The event was a huge success, spreading love and joy throughout the school.

Spitalfields Market

25th February 2025

This is our first market! The atmosphere was buzzing with energy, and there were loads of stalls showcasing their own creative ideas and products. The competition was intense, and although we didn't take home any awards, we learned something even more valuable: confidence. We used this experience to reflect and understand what we can improve for future markets.

Greenwich Market

19th March 2025

We took everything we learned from our last market and put it into action. With more confidence, better communication, and a stronger pitch, we connected with our customers more easily. We were thrilled to win the Best Product award and see our progress come to life.

East London Showcase

28th April 2025

We were proud to represent our team at the London East Showcase, where we presented our business to a panel of expert judges at BNY. It was an exciting but nerve-wracking experience, as we had to deliver a full presentation and answer questions about our product, mission, and progress so far. As a result, we won Best Presentation, Finalist, and Best Company. It was an unforgettable moment that showed us how far we've come and motivated us to keep going even stronger.

East London Final

13th May 2025

This was another great experience where our team members could gather an insight into the corporate world and experience a London business atmosphere. With more competition, we were nervous but we won the Best Company Runner Up Award at BCLP, a well-known law firm.

London & Essex Final

22th May 2025

We were so grateful to be able to visit the Amazon Headquarters to present to professional judges. With all the other teams being from private schools, we were pround to be the only state school that made it this far! But sadly all good things must come to an end, and we didn't win any awards. We are so grateful to everyone who supported us, and we learned a lot about business! Packet In!♻️✨
