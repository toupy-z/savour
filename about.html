About Us!

We are a passionate group of sixth-form students participating in the Young Enterprise program, dedicated to making a difference through sustainability. Our business transforms discarded crisp packets into innovative, reusable products, giving waste a new life.


You can find us at markets across London, where we share our vision for a greener future, one packet at a time. Join us on our journey towards sustainability and help us make an impact.
